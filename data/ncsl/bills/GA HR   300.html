House Resolution 300

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Denise N. Smith; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, Denise N. Smith is a visionary leader with over two decades of experience in technology and business strategy who currently serves as CEO of DNS Consulting, where she collaborates with small businesses, private equity, and venture capital portfolio companies to drive innovation, scalability, and transformative growth; and

WHEREAS, her distinguished career spans prior leadership roles at firms such as EY, Cox Automotive, and Hewitt Associates, during which time she has spearheaded initiatives to optimize performance, scale operations, and advance technological solutions; and

WHEREAS, a passionate advocate for leveraging technology to bridge societal gaps, Denise N. Smith has traveled to 152 countries, gaining a global perspective on business innovation and leadership, and her expertise in AI and technology transformation has positioned her as a forward-thinking trailblazer; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Denise N. Smith for her many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Denise N. Smith.
