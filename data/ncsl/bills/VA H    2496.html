HOUSE BILL NO. 2496

Offered January 8, 2025

Prefiled January 8, 2025

A BILL to amend the Code of Virginia by adding in Article 3 of Chapter 1 of Title 29.1 a section numbered 29.1-115, relating to Department of Wildlife Resources; use of a safety monitoring device; Fox Hill Boat Ramp.

Patron—Cordoza

Committee Referral Pending

Be it enacted by the General Assembly of Virginia:

1. That the Code of Virginia is amended by adding in Article 3 of Chapter 1 of Title 29.1 a section numbered 29.1-115 as follows:

§ 29.1-115. Use of a safety monitoring device; Fox Hill Boat Ramp.

The Department may place and operate a safety monitoring device at the Fox Hill Boat Ramp in the City of Hampton for the purpose of safety monitoring on state-owned property. For purposes of this section, "safety monitoring device" means a camera that uses machine learning to capture (i) license plate information or other visible characteristics of a vehicle and (ii) photographs, microphotographs, videotapes, or other recorded images.
