SENATE RESOLUTION

AMENDING THE RULES OF THE SENATE FOR THE YEARS 2023-2024

Introduced By: Senators McKenney, Gu, Ruggerio, Ciccone, Tikoian, Lawson, Zurier, LaMountain, Felag, and Urso

Date Introduced: January 14, 2025

Referred To: Recommended for Immediate Consideration

RESOLVED, That sections 5.1, 5.1-4 and 5.1-5 of the rules for the Senate adopted by the Senate at its January session A.D. 2023 entitled "Senate Resolution Adopting the Rules of the Senate for the Years 2023-2024" be and the same are hereby amended to read as follows:

SECTION 5.

COMMITTEES

5.1 Standing Committees.

The senate shall have the following standing committees:

1. Committee on Judiciary

2. Committee on Finance

3. Committee on Housing and Municipal Government

4. Committee on Labor and Gaming

5. Committee on Special Legislation and Veterans' Affairs

6. Committee on Health and Human Services

7. Committee on Education

8. Committee on Commerce

9. Committee on Environment and Agriculture

10. Committee on Rules, Government Ethics and Oversight

11. Committee on Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies

5.1-4 Committee on Labor and Gaming.

It shall be the duty of the committee on labor to consider the appointments of the governor referred to it by the senate which require the advice and consent of the senate and all legislation and matters relating to gaming issues, constitutional amendments relating to gaming issues, the workers' compensation and labor laws of the state, to maintain oversight over the application, execution and operation of the laws within its jurisdiction and to consider such other matters as may be referred to it by the senate.

5.1-5 Committee on Special Legislation and Veterans' Affairs.

It shall be the duty of the committee on special legislation and veterans' affairs to consider the appointments of the governor referred to it by the senate which require the advice and consent of the senate and all legislation and matters relating to constitutional amendments, liquor laws, gaming issues, license plates, veterans' affairs, domestic animals, commissions and resolutions, to maintain oversight over the application, execution and operation of the laws within its jurisdiction and to consider such other matters as may be referred to it by the senate.

5.1-11 Committee on Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies.

It shall be the duty of the committee on artificial intelligence and emerging technologies to consider the appointments of the governor referred to it by the senate which require the advice and consent of the senate and all legislation and matters relating to the creation, use, and regulation of artificial intelligence and emerging technologies, and to maintain oversight over the application, execution and operation of the laws within its jurisdiction and to consider such other matters as may be referred to it by the senate.

EXPLANATION

BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL

OF

SENATE RESOLUTION

AMENDING THE RULES OF THE SENATE FOR THE YEARS 2023-2024

This resolution would amend the rules of the Senate relative to committees and would create a new committee on artificial intelligence and emerging technologies.
