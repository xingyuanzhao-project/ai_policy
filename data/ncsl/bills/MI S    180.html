Sec. 2009. (1) From the funds appropriated in part 1 for alternative payment model transition, the department shall allocate $18,000,000.00 in general fund/general purpose revenue and any associated federal match or federal grant funding, to support the implementation of a Medicaid alternative payment methodology for FQHCs. The alternative payment methodology must be a population-based payment system that is based on a per-patient reimbursement for each Medicaid recipient assigned to each FQHC.
