AN ACT REQUIRING A STUDY OF THE EFFECT OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ON THE TRADES INDUSTRY.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:

That the general statutes be amended to require a study of the effect of artificial intelligence on trade workers. Such study shall include, but not be limited to, job displacement and the curriculum of the Technical Education and Career System.

Statement of Purpose:

To study the effect of artificial intelligence on the trades industry.
