AN ACT PROHIBITING THE USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE TO REPLACE TEACHERS.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:

That the general statutes be amended to prohibit the use of artificial intelligence to replace public school educators in providing instruction to and regular interaction with students.

Statement of Purpose:

To prohibit the use of artificial intelligence to replace public school educators in providing instruction to and regular interaction with students.
