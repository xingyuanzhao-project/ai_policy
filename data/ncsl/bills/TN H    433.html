HOUSE BILL 433

By Jones J

AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39, relative to unlawful images.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-318(a)(1), is amended by deleting the subdivision and substituting:

(1)

(A) The image was photographed or recorded under circumstances where the parties agreed or understood that the image would remain private; or

(B) The image of an identifiable person was created or modified by means of a computer software program, artificial intelligence application, or other digital editing tools; and

SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
