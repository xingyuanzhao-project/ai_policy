AN ACT PROHIBITING THE CREATION AND DISSEMINATION OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-GENERATED PORNOGRAPHIC IMAGES OF A PERSON WITHOUT CONSENT.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:

That the general statutes be amended to establish a crime prohibiting the creation and dissemination of artificial intelligence-generated pornographic images of a person without such person's consent.

Statement of Purpose:

To establish a criminal offense for the creation and dissemination of artificial intelligence-generated pornography.
