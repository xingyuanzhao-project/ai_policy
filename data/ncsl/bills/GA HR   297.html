House Resolution 297

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Laneisha Roberts; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, a proud alum of Florida State University, Laneisha Roberts is a seasoned corporate executive, entrepreneur, and workplace innovator with over 13 years of leadership experience in the bio-pharmaceutical, government, and healthcare sectors; and

WHEREAS, in 2023, she co-founded ReviewTailor, a Techstars-backed HR technology company that reimagines performance management through AI-powered solutions, making reviews faster, fairer, and more collaborative; and

WHEREAS, as a member of the inaugural AfroTech Founder Circle, Laneisha Roberts is committed to leveraging technology to break down workplace barriers, advance careers, and contribute to closing the wealth gap; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Laneisha Roberts for her many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Laneisha Roberts.
