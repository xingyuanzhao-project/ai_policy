House Resolution 313

By: Representatives Gilliard of the 162nd, Stephens of the 164th, Hitchens of the 161st, Jackson of the 165th, Westbrook of the 163rd, and others

A RESOLUTION

Honoring the inspiring journey and achievements of Nolan Sental Smith Jr.; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, Nolan Sental Smith Jr., born on January 18, 2001, in Savannah, Georgia, has shown the world what it means to dream big, work relentlessly, and rise above every challenge in pursuit of greatness; and

WHEREAS, from his early days on the football field in youth leagues to his standout performances at IMG Academy and Calvary Day School, Nolan's passion for the game and unwavering determination have been evident in every play he has made, earning him admiration and respect from coaches, teammates, and fans alike; and

WHEREAS, Nolan's time at the University of Georgia was nothing short of remarkable, where his relentless work ethic, leadership, and dedication to his team helped secure two College Football Playoff National Championships, a testament to his commitment to excellence and his pivotal role in the success of the Bulldogs; and

WHEREAS, Nolan's dream of playing in the NFL became a reality when he was selected by the Philadelphia Eagles in the first round of the 2023 NFL Draft, representing not only his talent but also the power of perseverance, hard work, and faith in himself; and

WHEREAS, beyond the field, Nolan is deeply committed to using his platform and influence to make a meaningful impact in his community and beyond, striving to be not only a football player but also a role model, leader, and change-maker who inspires others to dream big, work hard, and give back to those who need it most; and

WHEREAS, Nolan has touched countless lives through his dedication to inspiring and mentoring others, particularly through the Nolan Smith Foundation, which uplifts young athletes and provides them with the guidance to overcome obstacles, achieve their goals, and positively impact their communities; and

WHEREAS, Nolan's story is not just one of athletic triumph—it is a testament to the power of resilience and humility as well as the profound impact one person can have on the lives of those around them, especially through his support of his family and their shared journey together.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body honor Nolan Sental Smith Jr. for his extraordinary accomplishments, heart for service, and unwavering commitment to inspiring others to chase their dreams and make a positive difference in the world; extend their heartfelt congratulations for his outstanding contributions to the sport of football, dedication to uplifting others, and leadership both on and off the field; and wish him continued success and fulfillment in all his endeavors.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Nolan Sental Smith Jr.
