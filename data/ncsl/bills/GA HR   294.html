House Resolution 294

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Sherrod Davis; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, Sherrod Davis is a dynamic entrepreneur and tech executive who currently serves as the co-founder and CEO of EcoMap Technologies, an ecosystem intelligence platform that leverages data and analytics to support economic developers and ecosystem builders in driving entrepreneurial success and regional growth; and

WHEREAS, since joining EcoMap in 2021 as chief operating officer, he has played a pivotal role in its expansion, including the company's development of the Ecosystem Intelligence Platform—an AI-powered tool that centralizes and updates critical ecosystem data; and

WHEREAS, prior to his leadership at EcoMap, Sherrod Davis co-founded AdWap, an ad tech platform focused on charitable fund-raising; and

WHEREAS, his extensive experience in the tech industry further includes leadership roles at TrackOFF, a data privacy startup acquired by Avast Software in 2019, and Protenus, a Series D health tech startup recently acquired by Bluesight; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using his passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Sherrod Davis for his many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for his dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Sherrod Davis.
