H.C.R. No. 53

HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

WHEREAS, The Canyon High School FFA Ag Issues team won first place in the 2024 Texas FFA State Agricultural Issues Forum, held at Sam Houston State University; and

WHEREAS, The Agricultural Issues Forum Leadership Development Event is a contest where students research a current agricultural issue, create a portfolio, develop a presentation that outlines the pros and cons of their topic, and present their findings to a panel of judges; and

WHEREAS, The Canyon High School FFA Ag Issues team of Presley Davis, Lilli Klose, Gunnar McConlogue, Louden McConlogue, Evey McIntosh, and Allison Wade delivered an exceptional presentation on the use of artificial intelligence in agriculture; and

WHEREAS, Through countless hours of preparation and an unwavering commitment to excellence, the members of the Canyon High School FFA Ag Issues team have brought tremendous credit to their school and their community, and they may take justifiable pride in their outstanding accomplishments; now, therefore, be it

RESOLVED, That the 89th Legislature of the State of Texas hereby congratulate the Canyon High School FFA Ag Issues team on winning first place in the 2024 Texas FFA State Agricultural Issues Forum and extend to all those associated with the team sincere best wishes for continued success; and, be it further

RESOLVED, That an official copy of this resolution be prepared for the team as an expression of high regard by the Texas House of Representatives and Senate.
