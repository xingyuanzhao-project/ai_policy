Joint Resolution Regarding Price Fixing In Rental Housing

2025 GENERAL SESSION

STATE OF UTAH

Chief Sponsor: Nate Blouin

House Sponsor:

LONG TITLE

General Description:

This joint resolution calls on the Utah Attorney General to join the antitrust lawsuit against RealPage and landlords engaged in alleged price fixing actions.

Highlighted Provisions:

This resolution:

• highlights the U.S. Department of Justice case filed against RealPage and other landlords;

• outlines how the alleged collusion impacts Utah;

• lists the states already participating in the action;

• calls for a study; and

• urges the Utah Attorney General to join the antitrust lawsuit.

Money Appropriated in this Bill:

None

Other Special Clauses:

None

Be it resolved by the Legislature of the state of Utah:

WHEREAS, the U.S. Department of Justice has filed an antitrust action against RealPage and six major landlords alleging collusion in setting rental prices using shared data and algorithmic pricing;

WHEREAS, in Salt Lake County alone, these landlords manage over 20% of the multifamily rental units;

WHEREAS, the lawsuit alleges that by manipulating rental prices through shared information and algorithmic collusion, these landlords undermine fair market competition, drive rents higher than they should be, and ultimately make housing less affordable for Utah households;

WHEREAS, the Attorneys General of North Carolina, California, Colorado, Connecticut, Minnesota, Oregon, Tennessee, Washington, Illinois, and Massachusetts have already joined this action;

WHEREAS, the action against RealPage seeks to end these unlawful practices, promote transparency, and restore competition in the rental housing market; and

WHEREAS, participating in this case and studying the impacts on Utah's renters and the rental housing market of this price fixing would send a message that the Legislature is committed to protecting renters from abuse by powerful landlords:

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Legislature of the state of Utah urges the Utah Attorney General to join the antitrust lawsuit against RealPage and landlords engaged in these alleged price fixing actions.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Legislature shall study this issue and related matters in the appropriate 2025 interim committee.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a copy of this resolution be sent to the Utah Attorney General.
