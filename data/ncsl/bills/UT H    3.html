LONG TITLE

General Description:

This bill supplements or reduces appropriations otherwise provided for the support and operation of state government for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2024 and ending June 30, 2025.

Highlighted Provisions:

This bill:

▸ provides appropriations for the use and support of higher education and certain state agencies;

▸ authorizes full time employment levels for certain internal service funds;

▸ provides appropriations for other purposes as described; and

▸ provides intent language.

Money Appropriated in this Bill:

This bill appropriates $1,223,859,700 in operating and capital budgets for fiscal year 2025, including:

▸ $15,974,900 from General Fund; and

▸ ($260,100) from Income Tax Fund; and

▸ $1,208,144,900 from various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill appropriates ($21,438,200) in expendable funds and accounts for fiscal year 2025, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill appropriates $31,056,100 in business-like activities for fiscal year 2025, including:

▸ $1,750,000 from General Fund; and

▸ $260,100 from Income Tax Fund; and

▸ $29,046,000 from various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill appropriates ($6,893,200) in restricted fund and account transfers for fiscal year 2025, including:

▸ ($4,728,500) from General Fund; and

▸ ($2,396,500) from Income Tax Fund; and

▸ $231,800 from various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill appropriates $48,488,000 in transfers to unrestricted funds for fiscal year 2025, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill appropriates $362,868,100 in capital project funds for fiscal year 2025, all of which is from the various sources as detailed in this bill.

This bill reflects $44,067,100 in higher education budget reporting for fiscal year 2025.

Other Special Clauses:

This bill provides a special effective date.

This bill takes effect immediately.
