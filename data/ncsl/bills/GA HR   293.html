House Resolution 293

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Stacey Samedi; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, Stacey Samedi is the founder and product engineer of Samedi Label, an Atlanta-based technology company specializing in AI/ML integration and platform development; and

WHEREAS, at Samedi Label, she bridges her deep engineering expertise with strategic growth initiatives, specializing in user-centered design, systems integration, and data-driven decision-making to optimize technical infrastructure and drive product success; and

WHEREAS, her comprehensive approach to technical leadership, combined with her track record in both corporate and entrepreneurial environments, makes her a valuable contributor to Georgia's growing technology ecosystem; and

WHEREAS, Stacey Samedi has further demonstrated exceptional entrepreneurial success with Samedi Sneakers, generating over $100,000 in first-year organic sales; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Stacey Samedi for her many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Stacey Samedi.
