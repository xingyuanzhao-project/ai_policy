HR____ COMMENDING IGNITE AND ITS LEADERSHIP ON THEIR ACQUISITION OF RELIANT TECHNOLOGIES.

WHEREAS, with great pride and special pleasure, Ignite and its leadership are highly commended on their successful acquisition of Reliant Technologies; and

WHEREAS, both Ignite and Reliant Technologies are certified Service-Disabled, Veteran-Owned, Small Businesses who serve the defense and space industries in Huntsville; this acquisition enhances Ignite's ability to deliver cutting-edge solutions in artificial intelligence and machine learning (AI/ML), defense, and space operations, further strengthening their commitment to supporting the Warfighter and government partners; and

WHEREAS, Ignite delivers transformative solutions to solve their customers' impossible problems; the company's core capabilities include AI/ML, Multi-Domain Operations Support, Electromagnetic Spectrum Operations, Digital Transformation and Cyber, Space, and Programmatic Support; Reliant Technologies specializes in advanced digital system engineering and architecture, software development, and AI/ML solutions for defense and government agencies; with a team of expert engineers, developers, and innovators, they deliver analyses, models, and analytics that incorporate tailored secure, and compliant AI-driven tools that revolutionize system engineering, acquisition, logistics, and data analysis processes with customers like Missile Defense Agency, NASA, and the United States Army; and

WHEREAS, this acquisition is a partnership of two great companies that serve similar interests and customer bases; Ignite absorbed the leadership and employees of Reliant and Joe Hatchet, former CEO of Reliant, will serve Ignite as an Advisory Board member; the merging of the two companies increases Ignite's ability to deliver tailored solutions that address the unique needs of its customers; now therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That Ignite is commended and congratulated on its acquisition of Reliant Technologies and its personnel and this resolution is offered to its leadership in highest regard and with best wishes for continued prosperity and success.
