House Resolution 298

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Kristin Bell; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, a proud native of Little Rock, Arkansas, Kristin Bell is a senior human resources professional with a decade of experience spanning higher education, healthcare, finance, technology, and manufacturing; and

WHEREAS, in 2023, she co-founded ReviewTailor, a Techstars Portfolio Company that leverages AI to transform HR performance management, as well as CultureCite, an AI-driven HR platform designed to empower employees to advocate for themselves against workplace retaliation, discrimination, and harassment; and

WHEREAS, as the owner of CEEID HR Consulting LLC, she further partners with organizations as a trusted consultant with deep expertise in performance management, talent acquisition, DEI, leadership development, and organizational strategy; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Kristin Bell for her many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Kristin Bell.
