House Resolution 249

By: Representatives Beckles of the 96th, Adeyina of the 110th, Okoye of the 102nd, McClain of the 109th, Romman of the 97th, and others

A RESOLUTION

Recognizing and commending Splash Festivals; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, founded by Frances Schube and Cindy Flynn, Splash Festivals specializes in producing vibrant and engaging art events that promote the cities they represent, bringing together artisans, visitors, and local businesses in a mutual celebration of creativity and community; and

WHEREAS, since its incorporation in 2009, Splash Festivals has organized and produced such outstanding experiences as the Brookhaven Cherry Blossom Festival, the Acworth Art Fest, the Chamblee Art Fest, the Dunwoody Art Festival, the Alpharetta Arts Streetfest, the Norcross Art Splash Festival, and the Johns Creek Arts Festival; and

WHEREAS, the Norcross Art Splash Festival, one of the largest annual events in the City of Norcross, first began in 2004 and has since grown into a major attraction, drawing large crowds from both within and outside the city while also supporting local businesses and fostering economic activity in downtown Norcross; and

WHEREAS, the success of the Norcross Art Splash Festival is made possible through the dedication of more than 100 volunteers, many of whom are Norcross citizens, who generously contribute their time and effort to assist artisans, set up and break down booths, and welcome visitors with warmth and hospitality; and

WHEREAS, Splash Festivals works in close collaboration with the City of Norcross, including the police department and city staff, to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for festival attendees; and

WHEREAS, through their unique approach to event planning, Splash Festivals has established a strong reputation for producing high-quality festivals that engage communities, attract thousands of visitors, and celebrate the spirit of the arts; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this remarkable company be appropriately honored.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body recognize and commend Splash Festivals for its many outstanding contributions on behalf of the citizens of this state as well as extend best wishes for continued success.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Splash Festivals.
