STATE OF OKLAHOMA

1st Session of the 60th Legislature (2025)

HOUSE BILL 1899

By: May

AS INTRODUCED

An Act relating to artificial intelligence; enacting the Artificial Intelligence Act of 2025; providing for noncodification; and providing an effective date.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:

SECTION 1. NEW LAW A new section of law not to be codified in the Oklahoma Statutes reads as follows:

This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Artificial Intelligence Act of 2025".

SECTION 2. This act shall become effective November 1, 2025.
