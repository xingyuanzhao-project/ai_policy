House Resolution 295

By: Representative Kendrick of the 95th

A RESOLUTION

Commending Alicia Lyttle; and for other purposes.

WHEREAS, the second annual Tech Demo Day at the state capitol will be held March 13, 2025, hosted by Representative Dar'shun Kendrick; and

WHEREAS, this exciting event will both celebrate Women's History Month and highlight leaders from across the State of Georgia under the theme "Women In Artificial Intelligence"; and

WHEREAS, Alicia Lyttle currently serves as the CEO of AI Innovision, where she helps organizations implement AI-driven solutions to enhance efficiency, sustainability, and innovation; and

WHEREAS, widely known as the "Queen of AI," Alicia is the founder of the International Association of AI Consultants (IAAIC) and the creator of a leading AI certification program that equips professionals with the skills to apply AI in strategic procurement, sustainable practices, and more; and

WHEREAS, she has collaborated with top companies, marketing agencies, and government organizations to streamline operations, optimize resources, and build a future-ready approach to sustainability and strategic purchasing; and

WHEREAS, as an expert in AI education and professional development, Alicia also leads the AI Experts Club, a global community of individuals dedicated to advancing the practical use of AI; and

WHEREAS, it is abundantly fitting and proper that this outstanding participant be appropriately honored for using her passion and creativity to make our state a better place in which to live and work.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES that the members of this body commend Alicia Lyttle for her many remarkable career accomplishments as well as extend the most sincere gratitude for her dedicated efforts on behalf of the citizens of this state and beyond.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Clerk of the House of Representatives is authorized and directed to make an appropriate copy of this resolution available for distribution to Alicia Lyttle.
