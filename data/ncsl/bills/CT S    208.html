AN ACT PROHIBITING THE UNAUTHORIZED DISSEMINATION OF SYNTHETIC INTIMATE IMAGES OF ANOTHER PERSON.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:

That title 53a of the general statutes be amended to establish the crime of unlawful unauthorized dissemination of a synthetic intimate image of another person as a class A misdemeanor if the image is disseminated to one person and a class D felony if the image is disseminated to more than one person by means of an interactive computer service, information service or telecommunications service.

Statement of Purpose:

To prohibit unauthorized dissemination of certain synthetic images.
