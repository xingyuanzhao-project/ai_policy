104TH GENERAL ASSEMBLY

State of Illinois

2025 and 2026

SB1366

Introduced 1/28/2025, by Sen. Sally J. Turner

SYNOPSIS AS INTRODUCED:

New Act

Creates the State Government AI Act. Provides that, before January 1, 2028, the Department of Innovation and Technology shall adopt rules establishing policies and procedures concerning the development, procurement, deployment, use, and assessment of artificial intelligence by agencies of State government. Prohibits the deployment and use of artificial intelligence by agencies of State government, unless permitted by rules adopted by the Department. Requires certain impact assessment reports. Defines terms. Effective immediately.

A BILL FOR

AN ACT concerning State government.

Be it enacted by the People of the State of Illinois, represented in the General Assembly:

Section 1. Short title. This Act may be cited as the State Government AI Act.

Section 5. Definitions. In this Act:

"Agency of State government" means:

(1) any agency, department, or office in State government or any part of State government created by the Constitution of the State;

(2) any of the several agencies and departments of State government as designated in Section 5-15 of the Civil Administrative Code of Illinois; or

(3) any State agency, department, bureau, or office in the executive branch or legislative branch created by statute or executive order that does not exist as a constituent part of an agency, department, or office described under item (i) or item (ii) of this definition.

"Artificial intelligence" or "AI" means technology that enables computers and machines to simulate human learning, comprehension, problem solving, decision making, creativity, and autonomy.

Section 10. Policies and procedures. Before January 1, 2028, the Department of Innovation and Technology shall adopt rules establishing policies and procedures concerning the development, procurement, deployment, use, and assessment of artificial intelligence by agencies of State government to protect and enhance the health, welfare, and safety of the people of the State of Illinois. In developing these rules, the Department of Innovation and Technology may consult with the Generative AI and Natural Language Processing Task Force established under Section 1-80 of the Department of Innovation and Technology Act.

Section 15. Prohibition. Beginning January 1, 2028, unless permitted by rules adopted by the Department of Innovation and Technology, no agency of State government may deploy or use artificial intelligence.

Section 20. Impact assessment reports.

(a) On or before July 1, 2028, and on or before July 1 of every succeeding calendar year, every agency of State government shall submit an impact assessment report on the impact of artificial intelligence on that agency of State government to the Department of Innovation and Technology.

(b) On or before January 1, 2029, and on or before January 1 of every succeeding calendar year, the Department of Innovation and Technology shall submit an impact assessment report on the impact of artificial intelligence on State government to the General Assembly and the Governor.

Section 99. Effective date. This Act takes effect upon becoming law.
